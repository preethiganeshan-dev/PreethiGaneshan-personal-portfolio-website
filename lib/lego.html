Lego- A component library for personal portfolio website

This is a way to break down a given UX design into modular and self-sustaining components that can be reused across various platforms/sites to maintain one's personal theme/brand consistently. This approach to web development was taught by Tanay Pratap at neog bootcamp. Some industry examples of component libraries are Google's Material Design and Microsoft's Fluent Design.
